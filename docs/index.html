The Conscious Journey

My path from the foothills to the peaks of consciousness
2004-2015

Rooted in Himalayan Soil

My childhood in rural Nepal taught me the rhythms of nature and the wisdom of the earth. Growing up surrounded by mountains, I learned that true strength comes from stillness and connection to the land.

Key Learnings

  • Traditional farming practices passed down through generations
  • Importance of community interdependence
  • Respect for nature's cycles and seasons
  • Foundation for my spiritual connection to the earth

"The mountains taught me that growth comes from stillness, just as trees grow from solid roots."

2015-2020

Trekking Beyond the Mind

As I explored the Himalayas, I discovered that the greatest journeys are internal. Each step on the mountain became a step toward self-awareness, teaching me resilience and presence.

Spiritual Awakening

  • First exposure to yoga and meditation practices
  • Multi-day treks that became moving meditations
  • Learning from Himalayan yogis and spiritual guides
  • Developing my "Karma Yogi" philosophy of selfless action

"The mountain paths became my meditation cushions, the wind my mantra."

2020-2024

Code & Karma

Merging my technical skills with spiritual practice, I found harmony between the digital and natural worlds. Technology became a tool for connection rather than distraction.

Integration Phase

  • Studying management while deepening spiritual practice
  • Learning coding as a form of meditation and creation
  • Developing conscious technology principles
  • Early experiments with hemp clothing and sustainable business

"True innovation serves humanity without enslaving it to devices."

2024-Present

Japan & Conscious Entrepreneurship

Bringing my vision to Japan while deepening my commitment to conscious business, sustainable living, and spiritual practice.

Current Focus

  • Launching "Conscious Thread" hemp underwear brand
  • Developing plans for Himalayan Homestay & Trekking
  • Integrating Japanese minimalism with Nepali spirituality
  • Building tools to combat digital addiction

"My work serves my soul, rather than my soul serving work."

My Guiding Principles

The foundation of my Karma Yogi path

Seva (Selfless Service)

Work that serves humanity without expectation of reward. Creating value that uplifts communities and heals the planet.

Ahimsa (Non-Violence)

Harmony with all living beings through conscious choices. Sustainable practices that honor the earth and its inhabitants.

Santosha (Contentment)

Finding abundance in simplicity. Rejecting consumerism and the myth of scarcity. Living fully with less.

Svadhyaya (Self-Study)

Continuous inner exploration through meditation, breathwork, and mindful living. Knowing myself to better serve others.
